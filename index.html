Allow Me To Introduce Myself


I am a software engineer with a passion for creative-problem solving and self-driven learning. My love for learning new skills and honing a craft led me to become a Doctor of Music, Certified Personal Trainer, and ultimately a software engineer. Software engineering provides a means of combining the creativity I enjoyed working as a musician with the technical side that I enjoyed working as personal trainer. I hope to find a development role where I can continue to learn and bring creative projects to life.
